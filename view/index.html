About Us

Who we are..

The journey started in 2009 as DRHIHMAKX CO LTD solely engaged in music production.Now as an entertainment music company as it were,the need to launch out into the Internet space and the music business world arose with the advent of shooting a TV music reality show amongst other products of the company.

RHYTHM & LYRICS PROJECT is a GOSPEL MUSIC TV REALITY SHOW with a difference designed to converge vocalists and Instrumentalists in music competition where great rewards and awards are certain on our one big super stage down to the advent of several editions uniquely.

So,we scout and discover great latent talents through our live and online music auditions keeping in view great rewards and awards to the exceptionally talented music folks across Nigeria and Africa spreading and creating a niche globally.

Now you can join our Revamped Gospel Music Competition Awards (RGMCA 2024) 1ST EDITION.This is done Quarterly.
